// What is Scalpel?

South African dental practices lose thousands of rands each year to rejected medical aid claims, manual billing errors, and slow reconciliation. Scalpel cuts through the admin — giving your front desk a clean, fast tool to manage Discovery, Medimed, Bonitas, and other major schemes from a single desktop app. Built offline-first so it works whether your fibre is up or not.

01
Claims & invoicing
Full claim lifecycle from invoice creation to payment reconciliation. ICD-10 codes, procedure tariffs, shortfall tracking.
02
Offline-first
Works without internet. Syncs automatically across desktops when connected. Never lose data to a dropped connection.
03
SA-built
Designed for HPCSA compliance and local scheme formats. Understands dependent codes, membership numbers, and SA ID validation.
